Site Information

The Tassajara site was one of the later additions to the AREDN backbone. Previously there was a very intermittent connection between W6RR and Cuesta, which was prone to dropouts and instability due to a poor link quality. The addition of the Tassajara node helped bridge this gap and provide a better connection to users in the north, while also acting as fill in sector for clients that were too far south to be able to connect to the W6RR sectors.
